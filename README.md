# 🎄 Christmas Rhythm Game
**Christmas Rhythm Game** is a 2D rhythm prototype game on Unity for PC, created to study 2D game development. 
The project is made in a New Year's theme with all the famous rhythm game mechanics.

<img width="1500" height="840" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/70393637-8214-4d08-8907-484b37ca40db" />

## General description
The gameplay is based on the hitting-the-notes mechanics in the form of falling Christmas cookies. 
The cookies are falling into the plate in beat, and player must press the corresponding arrows strictly at the moment of touching with hit zone. 
The pressing direction is determined by the cookie's orientation

A score multiplier has been implemented. It increases with a series of normal/good/perfect hits without misses.
In addition to the multiplier, there is also a hit evaluation system that reads:
- Misses, 
- Normal hits, 
- Good hits, 
- Perfect hits.

And this time, all the visual assets, including the backgrounds and cookies, were entirely drawn in **Adobe Photoshop**.

## The game end
At the end of the game, when the music stops, a results screen is displayed. 

It calculates the number of hits of each type, the total accuracy percentage, the final score, and a total rank.
The rank is assigned depending on the total accuracy percentage.

## Visuals
Each hit has its own visual effects, Particle Systems and the game has Post-Processing.

Additionally, dynamic visual feedback has been created:
The background changes during the game based on the current rank and more cookies appear on the plate with each new rank.

For example, if the hit percentage is 100% and the game was played perfectly, 
then there'll be a mountain of cookies in the background and the player's rank would be the “SS” rank
